Russian Blue Cat

A Russian Blue cat helped inspire the Internet’s most famous 8-bit animated feline. Nyan Cat—the YouTube video-turned-viral Internet-meme of a flying cat-Pop Tart hybrid flying through space, leaving a rainbow trail in its wake—was created in 2011 by then-25-year-old illustrator Chris Torres, who owned a Russian Blue named Marty.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Avrage Lenght: 60cm
  • Avrage Weight: 2-7kg
  • Avrage Lifespan:10-20 years

Russian Blue cat is a curious and tranquil animal. They are known for their friendliness and intelligence, and are somewhat reserved. They have been known to play fetch, open doors, and are sensitive to human emotions. They enjoy playing with a variety of toys and develop loyal bonds to their loved ones and other family pets.
